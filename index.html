Account Deletion Request

Welcome to the Appointly account deletion page. We value your privacy and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an Email: Please email us at hadihawi.developer@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include your account details and any relevant information.
  2. Verification: Our support team will verify your identity and may request additional details if needed.
  3. Processing: After verification, your account will be scheduled for deletion. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Data Deletion and Retention Policy

Data Deleted: Upon account deletion, your personal data—such as your name, email address, profile details, and appointment history—will be removed.

Data Kept: Some non-identifiable data (including anonymized usage statistics and log files) may be retained for internal analytics and compliance purposes.

Retention Period: Certain information may be retained for up to 30 days post-deletion to meet legal and regulatory obligations.
